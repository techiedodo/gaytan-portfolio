Case Study

Summary
Dr. Mishra was looking to give his business website, Chatsworth Dental Group, a face lift. The current website was created in 2011 and contained a large amount of content. The overall feel of the website was dated and not mobile responsive. Looking at the his competitor's websites, Dr. Mishra wanted to ensure that his new site stood out.

Case Study Overview

The Challenge

The decision to update the website was clear. The challenge came when looking at the overall design. Looking at competitor sites, we needed to make sure that the new design was modern, but not too overpowering.

The Solution

The new design for the website was a single page site. The aim was to provide something succinct and appealing to new customer as well as current ones. Mobile responsiveness was a priority.

The Result

The page was divided into four section: hero, services, staff, and new patients. The main goal was to keep the site fresh with a focus on appealing to new customers.


The "New Patient Forms" feature allows new patients to complete forms online in order to expedite processing thereby decreasing idle time at the office. Streamlining patient forms is aimed at improving guest service. This single page layout placed the Contact Form front and center making it easier to locate and use. The responsive design translated to a better user experience when viewing on a mobile device.

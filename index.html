Case Study

Summary
Dr. Mishra was looking to give his business website a face lift. The current website was created in 2011 and contains a large amount of content. The overall feel of the current website was dated and and not mobile responsive.

Case Study Overview

The Challenge

The decision to update the website was clear. The challenge came when looking at the overall design. Looking at competitor site, we needed to make sure that the new design was modern, but not too overpowering.

The Solution

The new design for the website was changed to be a single page site. The decision was made in order to provide something succinct and appealing to new customer as well as current ones. Mobile responsiveness was also a priority.

The Result

A new feature was added that allowed new patients to complete forms online. The goal of having this feature was to expedite the processing of patients thereby decreasing the idle time at the office. This single page layout also put the contact form front and center making it easier to locate. The responsive design also translated to a better user experience when viewing the site on a mobile device.
